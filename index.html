How users feel about their experiences interacting with chatbots

People enjoy using chatbots for simple requests

Customers are inherently busy people. As a result of more purchases being made online, consumers are interacting with chatbots at much higher rates. Some experiences are better than others and sometimes chatbots fail to deliver. Research suggests that most consumers are happy to use chatbots for simple requests. Gone are the days where waiting on hold for extended periods of time was expected. Company chatbots are here to save the day! Or at least we’d like to hope so...
